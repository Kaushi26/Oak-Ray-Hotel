OAK RAY

HOTELS IN SRI LANKA

Spanning across the scenic spots of this island paradise, Oak Ray properties are located in culturally significant Kandy, Dambulla and Sigiriya in the central region, in the hilly plains of Ramboda and Nuwara Eliya, and in the picturesque Tissamaharama of the South.


Kandy

Oak Ray Regency, Kandy.

The Hotel boasts of wide open spaces interspersed with seating areas for casual chatter. The rooftop pool is especially spectacular with views of the city and surrounding mountains, this 4 Star property facilitates city style living complete with contemporary luxury with ten banquet halls on separate floors to lavishly host conferences and events.

Kandy

Senani Hotel, Kandy.

Overlooking a lake, Senani Hotel is located in scenic Kandy and is surrounded by tropical greenery. The beautiful hotel features a large outdoor swimming pool, a tour desk and free Wi-Fi in public areas.

Read More

Kandy

Oak Ray City Hotel, Kandy.

Dressed in teak and stone interior, the Hotel overlooks the famous Hanthane mountain range. Conveniently situated in the center of Kandy, this 3 Star property facilitates city style living complete with contemporary luxury, while being just at the brink of your Kandyan adventure.

Read More

Sigiriya

Fresco Water Villa, Sigiriya.

Our Villa stands atop a waterway that flows beneath the pleasant green structure. Vibrantly coloured tropical fish swim happily in the channel, alongside spurts of river reeds. With the waterway, the sun dappled pool on one side and a lily pond on the other, the property is a beautiful piece of art.

Read More

Dambulla

Pelwehara Village Resort, Dambulla.

Overlooking lush paddy fields and surrounded by large trees that spread their branches over the property, this eco-friendly Resort is always kept cool despite the humid weather. Wild blossoms, vines that trellis over, canopies of flowering plants adorning the thatched hut built outdoors for seating, all add to its irresistible charm.

Ramboda

Oak Ray Tea Bush, Ramboda.

Tea Bush – Ramboda is an ideal romantic getaway. The property flaunts expansive glass walls, large French windows, decks that offer views of the cascades, and minimalist decor that accentuates the gorgeous mountainside

Nuwara Eliya

Oak Ray Summerhill Breeze, Nuwara Eliya.

Summerhill Breeze is the perfect love affair between serenity and beauty. Surrounded by Pine and Cypress trees, a welcome trail of blue hydrangeas lead the way up to the property, from where the restaurant generously offers a 180° view of the mountains.

Tissamaharama

Oak Ray Lake Resort, Tissa.

The Resort sits facing the Tissa Lake with its teeming wild and birdlife. It enjoys spectacular views when the sun sets in the middle of the Lake, caressing the vicinity with its glorious pink hue. Set amidst this scenic backdrop, lounging in the Resort’s infinity pool is a blissful intimation with nature.
