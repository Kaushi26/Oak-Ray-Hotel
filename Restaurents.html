DINE-INN's IN SRI LANKA

Set out in al fresco style, designed as a dock layout, or simply offering large indoor dining halls, our Restaurants offer a gastronomical journey across the world that takes guests on delectable culinary adventures.


OAK RAY RESTAURANT - KANDY

The city of Kandy has much to offer, Oak Ray Restaurant, conveniently located in the city, is an ideal spot to grab a quick lunch in the middle of business meetings, or while on a leisurely sightseeing vacation

The Restaurant serves an extensive variety of dishes from across the globe. Our international cuisine ensures everyone has a taste of their all time favourite preparation, or a bite of their comfort food.

OAK RAY HERITAGE BOUTIQUE - KANDY

The Wallauwwa accentuates some of the specialties of Sri Lanka. May it be world acclaimed Ceylon Tea, exquisitely set gemstone jewellery, wood carvings, handloom clothes and household items, marble ornaments, figurines of deities or scented candles; there is some souvenir for everyone to take home.

Gourmet finger food and coffees are our specialty here at the Oak ray Heritage. Guests could order their favourite fare from our a la carte menu.

Visit Menu

SENANI RESTAURANT - KANDY

Senani Restaurant is a rooftop dining area that offers a bird's eye view of the charming city of Kandy, alongside delectable culinary options.

Guest could opt to feast on the international buffet spread at the Restaurant, or alternatively, hand-pick their favourite dishes from our a la carte menu.

Visit Menu

OAK RAY FLOWER DRUM - COLOMBO

Oak Ray flower drum restaurant is an oasis in the heart of Colombo & an ideal venue to relax with friends. Dine at this trending location to enjoy greater variety and quality like never before.

Located in the heart of Colombo, The Restaurant offers an authentic taste of the Orient. It serves up Chinese favourites that are relished around the globe.

Visit Menu

OAK RAY ANCHORAGE - GALLE

Paying tribute to the handicrafts of the South, Oak Ray Anchorage is a unique property tastefully decorated with local demon masks, wood carvings and other colourful trinkets on display.

Located in the fascinating city of Galle, Oak Ray Anchorage offers a delectable variety of international cuisine to its guests. The Restaurant specializes in lunchtime fare.

OAK RAY RESTAURANT
- RAMBODA

Oak Ray Restaurant is a haven to stop by on a long drive up country, and have a warm and delicious meal.

This mountainside restaurant offers a variety of delectable dishes from around the globe.

ISIWARA AYURVEDIC VILLAGE - MATALE

Isiwara is a novel concept of a self sustaining village that runs its own Restaurant, and Herb and Spices boutique. The village grows its own food crops, and even has a factory to produce and freshly process the ingredients required for the management of the Restaurant and Boutique.

The restaurant serves international cuisine with an emphasis on authentic Sri Lankan delicacies.
