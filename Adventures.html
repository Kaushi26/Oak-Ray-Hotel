Holistic Activities

Staying at the Oak Ray Hotels located around this beautiful paradise of Sri Lanka is a rewarding sensual adventure. From historic sites, to parks and sanctuariesthere there is much more to offer

Sigiriya

Sigiriya is a culturally significant town in the annals of Sri Lankan history. It is also a UNESCO listed world heritage site. Besides the lion shaped rock fortress that is both an architectural wonder and an ode to art, Sigiriya also offers easy access to other culturally acclaimed sites in the country, and to intriguing wildlife sanctuaries.

Activities to do:

  • Sigiriya Rock Fortress
  • Elephant back ride
  • Ritigala Forest
  • Dambulla Golden Cave Temple
  • Minneriya National Park
  • Pidurangala Rock

Kandy

The city of Kandy is not just an epicenter of culture and religious wonder, it also serves as a central point from which to visit some of the country's fascinating wildlife and historic monuments. The Hotel arranges tours and excursions to sites of interest.

Activities to do:

  • Temple of the Sacred Tooth
  • Botanical gardens
  • Udawattekele Sanctuary
  • Hanthana Mountain Range
  • Tea Museum

Ella

Ella in Sri Lanka is a small laid-back town surrounded by the beautiful greens of tea. There are a lot of things to do in Ella. Take one of the most beautiful train rides from Nuwara Eliya to Ella and fall in love with the surroundings. You will probably end up staying longer than you actually planned to. Ella has a comfortable climate where the nights are cool and the daytime around 28 degrees.

Activities to do:

  • Climb up Little Adam’s peak
  • Tea Field Views at Lipton’s Seat
  • Walk along the rails on the Nine Arch Bridge in Ella
  • Get a fresh shower at the Ravana Falls
  • Hike up to the top of Ella Rock
  • Swim at Diyaluma Falls, 2nd highest waterfall in Sri Lanka

Tissamaharama

The Resort, located opposite the Tissa Lake, affords many opportunities to launch on little boat rides, and escapades that are thoroughly satisfying. Besides these charming options, visitors could also opt to visit the neighboring national parks to sight fascinating and endemic wildlife.

Activities to do:

  • Trekking
  • Yala National Park
  • River bath
  • Camping
  • Kirinda Viharaya
  • Bird Watching
