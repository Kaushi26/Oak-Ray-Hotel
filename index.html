About Us

  • Founded in the year 2002 by the young entrepreneur Sujeewa Palliyaguruge, the Oak Ray Group specializes in Hotel & Restaurant management, and the development of indigenous crafts.

  • With a mission to create a complete Sri Lankan experience for travelers, today it owns a number of Hotels and Resorts across the beautiful landscapes of Sri Lanka.

About Our Branches

Oak Ray Wild Yala, Tissamaharama

A premier among Yala hotels, Oak Ray Wild Yala is situated at Tissamaharama, in close proximity to the Yala wildlife sanctuary. The hotel consists of 44 Superior Deluxe Rooms with modern amenities. The elegant restaurant and bar offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape garden. The pool secluded amongst the landscape garden is a vantage point to soak in spectacular views of the sunset.

Oak Ray Haridra,
Wadduwa

Oak Ray Beach Hotel is an exclusive beach front hotel situated in Wadduwa and one of the popular resorts among Wadduwa Beach Hotels. Situated in a magnificent beach with breathtaking sea view along the West Coast of Sri Lanka, designed to be the smart choice for both leisure and business travelers, with a unique experience of leisure and tranquility combined with a choice of luxurious experience being offered

Oak Ray Gap,
Ella

Oak Ray Ella Gap a popular Ella hotel welcomes guests with luxury rooms, an outdoor swimming pool and restaurant / bar. The property also provides free Wi-Fi. The property is located at the Ella city centre and is in close proximity to the scenic Ravana Waterfalls, Nine Arches Bridge Little Adam’s Peak and the famous Ella Rock.

About Our Restaurents

Oak Ray Flower Drum, Colombo

Oak Ray flower drum restaurant is an oasis in the heart of Colombo & an ideal venue to relax with friends. The warm hospitality of our staff combined with the breathtakingly stylish decoration of our restaurant will ensure you a memorable dining experience, time and time again.

Isiwara Ayurvedic Village, Matale

Isiwara is a novel concept of a self sustaining village that runs its own Restaurant, and Herb and Spices boutique. The village grows its own food crops, and even has a factory to produce and freshly process the ingredients required for the management of the Restaurant and Boutique.

Oak Ray Anchorage, Galle

Paying tribute to the handicrafts of the South, Oak Ray Anchorage is a unique property tastefully decorated with local demon masks, wood carvings and other colourful trinkets on display.
